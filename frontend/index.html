Convexity searches the eligible universe of U.S. small- and micro-cap companies and ranks the highest-conviction opportunities using twelve independent, transparent lines of evidence — value, growth, quality, financial health, technical structure, momentum, catalysts, risk, management, competitive position, ownership, and historical analogs. Every score traces back to the data that produced it.

Research tool — not investment advice, and not a predictor. No system can reliably forecast stock prices. Convexity aggregates public evidence into transparent, auditable scores to support your own research. High conviction here means many independent signals agree — never a guarantee. Do your own due diligence.
