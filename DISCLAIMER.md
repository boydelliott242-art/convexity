# Disclaimer

**Convexity is a research and screening tool. It is not investment advice, and it is not a
predictor of stock prices or returns.**

- **No predictive guarantee.** No system — including this one — can reliably predict stock
  moves. Convexity does not forecast prices, does not promise returns, and must never be
  described as doing so. Markets are adaptive and partly unpredictable; past patterns do not
  guarantee future results.

- **What Convexity actually does.** It transparently aggregates many *independent* pieces of
  evidence (valuation, growth, quality, financial health, catalysts, risk, management,
  competitive position, ownership, technicals, momentum, historical analogs) into explainable
  0–100 sub-scores and a composite score. Every point of every score traces back to named,
  dated, sourced `Evidence`. The output is an organised starting point for human research,
  not a decision.

- **Conviction requires agreement.** A high composite score is only treated as high-conviction
  when *many independent categories agree*. A single extreme signal is explicitly down-weighted.
  The reported `signal_agreement` and `conviction_confidence` exist so a reader can see how much
  genuine, corroborated evidence stands behind a result.

- **Missing data lowers confidence; it is never invented.** Small- and micro-cap securities
  frequently lack coverage for some metrics. When data is missing it is marked missing, the
  affected sub-score's confidence and data-coverage are reduced, and the gap is recorded in
  `data_warnings`. Convexity never fabricates a number to fill a hole.

- **Micro-cap risk.** Small- and micro-cap equities can be illiquid, volatile, thinly covered,
  and subject to elevated fraud and manipulation risk. Screening results can be wrong or stale.

- **Do your own diligence.** Nothing here is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any
  security. Consult a qualified, licensed financial professional and verify all data
  independently before making any decision. You are solely responsible for your own decisions.

  in the project [LICENSE](LICENSE).
