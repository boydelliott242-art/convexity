# Convexity

Convexity is an institutional-grade, evidence-driven research and screening platform for
small-cap and micro-cap equities. It is a **research and screening tool, not a predictor and
not investment advice** — no system can reliably predict stock moves. Its value is the
transparent aggregation of many *independent* pieces of evidence (value, growth, quality,
financial health, catalysts, risk, ownership, and more) into explainable 0–100 scores that a
human analyst can audit end to end. High conviction is asserted only when many independent
signals agree, and missing data lowers confidence rather than being fabricated.
